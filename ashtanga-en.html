MYSORE

Ashtanga Yoga · Traditional Practice

Mysore is the most traditional way to practice Ashtanga Yoga. Each student practises a fixed sequence at their own pace and from memory, whilst the teacher provides individualised guidance and adjustments.

Teaching Features

Traditional Method

Based on the traditional Ashtanga practice method

Individual Attention

Practice adjustments based on individual circumstances and progress

Continuous Refinement

Cultivate patience and inner awareness through repetitive practice

Practice Style

Mysore

Self-Practice · Individualised Adjustments

  • Flexible arrival within a set time frame
  • Memorise and practise the fixed sequence at your own pace
  • Teacher provides real-time adjustments based on the individual
  • Develop self-practice ability and inner awareness
  • Suitable for practitioners of all levels

Led Class

Unified Pace · Guided Practice

  • Group practice starts on time
  • Teacher guides the entire sequence using Sanskrit count
  • Fixed pace and breath coordination
  • Deepen coordination between postures and breath
  • Suitable for practitioners with a foundation
